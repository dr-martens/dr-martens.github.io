Biography

I am a Theoretical Physicist (PhD) by training with more than 12 years of experience in statistical data analysis, mathematical modeling of complex nonlinear systems and stochastic processes. Autor of 15+ scientific publications with vast experience in scientific presentation, mentoring and communication in interdisciplinary environments. I use my continuously developing, strong programming skills in Python, R, Matlab and C to solve challenging problems based on data sets, to develop models and related markers for estimates and forecasts.

I am looking for opportunities to apply my deep knowledge of data analysis and programming to help companpies use data to achieve their business objectives.
